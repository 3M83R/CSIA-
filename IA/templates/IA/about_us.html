Welcome to Stay at Chiang Rai!

We made this website so that the hostels arround the Chaing Rai province in the Northern Thailand could be recognised by more and more tourists.
The main objective of this website is to help the local hostels, so there won't be payment online. We only offer the chance of booking.
Regarding the payment of the hostels, it will change depending on the differnet hostels. No worries though, for we will list the options for payments with different hostels. This started as idea just to help out those hostels which has little to no knowledge of how to be discovered.


Due to the geolocation of Chiang Rai, not many tourist goes to this location for their vacation in Thailand.
